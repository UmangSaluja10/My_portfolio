GradeBoost

GradeBoost is a comprehensive educational app designed for CBSE Class 10th and 12th students. With a user-friendly interface and a focus on enhancing academic performance, GradeBoost offers a wide range of features to support students in their learning journey. The app includes detailed solved exercises, practice sheets for key topics, and sample papers that replicate the real exam format to help students prepare effectively. Additionally, the built-in calculator allows for quick and easy calculations, making study sessions more efficient. GradeBoost aims to simplify complex concepts and provide students with the resources they need to succeed in their exams. Whether it's practicing important questions or preparing for finals, the app offers everything a student needs to excel academically. Its easy-to-navigate design ensures a seamless experience, while its comprehensive content covers everything required to boost student performance. GradeBoost is the perfect study companion, combining essential learning tools in one place to empower students in their academic journey.

Semester Project

Nature's Pulse is a serene and informative blogging platform dedicated to exploring the beauty, intricacies, and wonders of the natural world. The website is designed to be a space where nature enthusiasts, environmentalists, and curious minds can delve into various topics about the environment, wildlife, sustainability, and outdoor adventures. Through insightful articles, vibrant photography, and engaging stories, Nature's Pulse brings the essence of nature directly to its readers. From detailed explorations of forest ecosystems and mountain ranges to discussions on environmental conservation efforts and the importance of preserving biodiversity, this platform aims to raise awareness and inspire action towards protecting our planet. Whether it’s sharing the awe-inspiring beauty of wildlife, revealing the hidden secrets of forests, or offering tips on how to live a more eco-friendly lifestyle, Nature's Pulse encourages readers to connect with the environment and feel its pulse through every article. It’s a blog that aims to unite readers with the healing and captivating power of the natural world.
